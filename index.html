Welcome

Welcome to the official 2b2t shop and thank you for visiting.

Here you can purchase priority queue status for your account, which will let you join the 2b2t server faster.

The priority queue status lasts for one month, after which it expires and is removed from your account.

About

2b2t is a minecraft server with the goal to never reset the world in a free for all no rules pvp environment, with some modification to the vanilla survival gamemode.

The world is 12 years 9 months old, with a size of 23 000 GBs and over 821 220 players visiting at least once. The IP to connect is 2b2t.org

The server is on Minecraft Java version 1.19 and we're currently working on making it possible to update the server to 1.20. Thank you for your support.
